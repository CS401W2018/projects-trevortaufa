3. What are special entity characters?

Special entity tags are tags that are used to edit how texts look on our webpages.

4. What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags are tags that space out text and has more so to do with the layout of the text rather than the actual text itself.

5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL are Ordered Lists and UL are Unordered List. The names are quite self explanatory but ordered lists give "rank" and puts the list in order whereas unordered lists are lists that have no rank and is quite simply a list

Ordered lists can have add attributes to make different types of tags like numbers, letters (upper or lowercase) and even Roman Numerals (also can use upper or lowercase).
