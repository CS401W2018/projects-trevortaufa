What are structural tags?

  • What goes in the header?

    The header generally has things like:

    • Logo
    • The site's main title or subtitle
    • A navigation menu (optional)
    • A search bar or introductory content relevant to the page

  • What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

    The nav can go in the header but it doesn't have to. The nav can also be placed in the sidebar or a separate footer navigation.

  • What goes in Main?

    The main contains the primary content of the document, such as:

    • Articles, blog posts, or product descriptions
    • Forms or interactive elements
    • Images, videos, or galleries
    • Anything that is the central focus of the page.

  • What goes in the Footer?

    The footer can include:

    • Copyright notices
    • Contact details (e.g., email or phone number)
    • Social media links
    • Secondary navigation
    • Disclaimers, privacy policies or terms of use

  • HTML Validation

    HTML validation ensures that your HTML code follows the rules and standards defined by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). Validatiing your code improves accessibility, SEO , and browser compatibility.

    • How to validate:
      1. Use and online validator like W3C Markup Validation Service.
      2. Input your HTML code or URL into the tool.
      3. Review and fix any errors or warnings it reports.
    • Why validate:
      • Prevents errors that might break your design.
      • Ensures consistent behaviour across browsers
      • Enhances user experience and accessibility
