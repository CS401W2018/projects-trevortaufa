Interest Hero

Hakeem Hoang

Henry Hoang is a Vietnamese Muslim barber and the owner of Barber Accelerator. He grew up as an immigrant in Australia and became a barber after quitting medical school. He became popular on TikTok for posting "before vs after" style content. Hakeem has shared is story of converting to Islam and how it has resonated with him as he's fasted during Ramadan, allowing him to see himself for who he is.

Muslim
A person who follow the religion of Islam, believing in one God (Allah) and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad as conveyed in the Qur'an.

Fasting
The act of abstaining from food, drinkm smoking and other physical needs for a specific period, often for religious or spiritual reasons. In Islam, fasting is key practice during Ramadan.

Ramadan
The ninth month of the Islamic calendar, observed my Muslims worldwide as a holy month of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community. During Ramadan, Muslims fast from dawn to sunset and focus on growth, charity, and worship.
