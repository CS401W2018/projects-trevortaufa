SpongeBob SquarePants is a happy-go-lucky, energetic sea sponge who live in a pineapple under the sea. He works as a fry cook at the Krust Krab, where he is known for his enthusiasm and love for making Krabby Patties. Despite his childlike innocence, SpongeBob often finds himself in humorous and adventurous activities with his friends, Patrick and Squidward.

Patrick Star is Spongebob's best friend, know for his simple-mindedness and laid-back attitude. He live under a rock next in Bikini Bottom and enjoys spending his days doing nothing but lounging and eating. Despite his lack of intelligencem Patrick oftens provides comic relief with his quirky and unpredictable behavior.

Squidward Tentacles is SpongeBobs' grumpy and sarcastic neighbor, who works as a cashier at the Krusty Krab. He has a passion for playing the clarinet and art, though he often feels unappreciated and frustrated by his surroundings. Squidward's disdain for Spongebob and Patrick's antics often leads to humorous situations, highlighitng is misanthropic nature.
