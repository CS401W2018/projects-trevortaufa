Trevor Taufa

Aloha! My name is Trevor Taufa. I am of Tongan descent and was born and raised in Australia. I am the 10th child of 13 children. There was always something to do growing up, and always something to play with or fight with (depending on what mood you're in.)

Just like a lot of my siblings, we grew up playing a lot of sports. From what I can remember, my father always worked at least 2 jobs at a time to support the family financially. Even though he was a busy man working many jobs, he still managed to have time to take me to a lot of sports games. He'd take me on intercity trips to play rugby tournaments and was at Rugby games every weekend without fail.


Sports I've played competitively (in chronological order)

  1. Rugby Union
  2. Soccer
  3. Rugby League
  4. Track
  5. American Football
