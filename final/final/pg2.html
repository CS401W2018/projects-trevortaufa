Salary & Job Market

What Kind of Salary you can Expect

The field of computer science offers competitive salaries across a wide range of roles. Entry-level positions often start around $60,000-$80,000 annually, and with more and more experience you can earn well over $100,000. Loaction, experience, and industry all play key roles in salary levels.

Job Role Entry-Level Salary Mid-Level Salary Senior-Level Salary
Software Developer $70,000 $95,000 $130,000+
Data Scientist $80,000 $110,000 $150,000+
Cybersecurity Analyst $75,000 $100,000 $140,000+
Web Developer $60,000 $85,000 $120,000+
AI/ML Engineer $90,000 $125,000 $160,000+

The demand for copmuter scince professionals continues to grow rapidly. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics:

  • Software development jobs are projected to grow 25% by 2032, much faster than the average for all occupations.
  • Cybersecurity roles are in high deman due to increasing concerns about data privacy and digital security.
  • AI and machine Learning are among the fastest-growing areas in tech, with companies seeking talent to innovate and stay competitive.
