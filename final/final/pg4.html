Success Stories

From Student to Software Engineer - Sarah's Journey

Sarah Martinez started her computer science journey in college with little programming experience. After taking on a summer internship, she discovered a passion for backend development. Today, she's a software engineer at Google, where she works on scalable cloud solutions. Her advice to students? "Never be afraid to ask questions and build projects that excite you."

Coding Her Way to Cybersecurity - Aisha's Path

Aisha Chen took a cybersecurity elective in her second year of college and instantly fell in love with the challenge of ethical hacking. After completing a cybersecurity bootcamp and earning her CompTIA Security+ certification, she landed a job with a major financial firm, protecting their systems from digital threats.

Data Science with a Purposes - Jason's Impact

Jason Kim combined his love of statistics and social good by becoming a data scientist for a non-profit organization. He uses machine learning to analyze public health trends and help undeserved communities get better access to care. "Computer science lets me do meaningful work every day," he shares.
