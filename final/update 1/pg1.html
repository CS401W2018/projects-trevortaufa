Career Paths

Explore your Options in Computer Science

A degree in computer science opens the door to many exciting career paths. Whether you love coding, analyzing data, designing systems, or solving real-world problems, there's a place for you in tech.

Popular Career Paths

Here are some of the most common and in-demand careers for computer science graduates:

  • Software Developer: Build apps, websites, and software used by millions.
  • Web Developer: Focus on front-end (what users see) or back-end (server-side logic).
  • AI/Machine Learning Engineer: Create intelligent systems that can learn and adapt.
  • Game Developer: Design code and interactive games for consoles, PCs, or mobile devices.
  • Mobile App Developer: Build apps for iOS and Android platforms.
  • UX/UI Designer: Create user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences.
